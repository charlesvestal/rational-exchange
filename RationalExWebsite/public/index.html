An Exchange Calculator with Cultural Context

Letting you rationalize purchases worldwide...

Rational Exchange is an exchange calculator that takes into account cultural context and is the easiest way to figure out "how much would they charge me back home?", considering what you'd pay in tax and tip wherever you're visitng, and wherever you're from.

  • Plain English
